Welcome to ONI

ONI is a clan comprised of passionate gamers who are eager to expand the ideals of a military-based clan. We are both a group of friends and a Milsim group with the goal of providing a fun and safe gaming experience while teaching players high-level skills for the game. We emphasize teamwork and learning game mechanics, all while incorporating aspects of intelligence gathering and military structure.

Clan History

We were established in 2014 on Halo Reach with a gathering of friends who initially sought a group capable of providing the UNSC with accurate intelligence. Over time, during Project Blacklight, I was selected as the Commander-in-Chief (CINC) to oversee the Midwest Office of Naval Intelligence. After nine years, the Midwest group has achieved a significant milestone by becoming the only team to officially develop a replica of the UNSC Spartan II Conscription files, originally created by Dr. Halsey and recreated under my leadership as CINC Ava.

Mission: Reconnaissance Operation

Objective: Gather intelligence on enemy positions and activities in a specific region of the game map. Provide detailed reports to enhance our strategic advantage.

Mission: Training Exercise

Objective: Conduct regular training sessions to improve teamwork, communication, and tactical skills. Enhance our ability to work together effectively during missions.

Mission: Talent Recruitment

Objective: Continuously search for skilled players within the sub fleets under the UNSC. Identify and recruit talented individuals to strengthen our forces.
